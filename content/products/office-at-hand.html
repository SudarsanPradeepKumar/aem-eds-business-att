Frequently asked questions

What is AT&T Office@Hand?

AT&T Office@Hand is a cloud-based unified communications platform that combines business phone, video conferencing, team messaging, and fax in a single solution. It works on desktop, mobile devices, and desk phones.

How much does Office@Hand cost?

AT&T Office@Hand starts at $25 per user per month for the Standard plan. Premium is $35/user/month and Ultimate is $45/user/month. Volume discounts are available for larger teams.

Can I keep my existing business phone number?

Yes, you can port your existing business phone numbers to AT&T Office@Hand at no additional cost. The porting process typically takes 2-4 weeks.

Does Office@Hand work with my existing business tools?

Yes, AT&T Office@Hand integrates with over 300 business applications including Microsoft 365, Google Workspace, Salesforce, Zendesk, and many more.

What devices can I use with Office@Hand?

AT&T Office@Hand works on Windows and Mac computers, iOS and Android mobile devices, and compatible desk phones. You can also use it directly in your web browser.
