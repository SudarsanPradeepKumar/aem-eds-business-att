## Frequently asked questions

**Why should I choose AT&T over other business internet providers?**

There are plenty of reasons to choose AT&T as your ISP for your business. Our internet has over 99% proven reliability and it's fast and secure. You can count on our dedicated 24/7 business-class customer care for fast and attentive solutions.

**What business Wi-Fi solutions are available?**

AT&T Business Internet services provide Wi-Fi solutions for businesses, with Wi-Fi gateways included in Fiber Internet plans, and optional add-on Wi-Fi equipment available with Dedicated Internet, Fixed Wireless (Internet Air), and Wireless Broadband services.

**What small business internet services are available?**

AT&T Business offers reliable small business internet services like AT&T Business Fiber to keep your team connected and enhance your business's performance.

**What is a good internet speed for my business?**

A good internet speed is dependent on your needs. Internet plans are measured by download and upload speeds. When choosing the best internet plan for your business, consider which ISPs and internet plans are available in your area, how you plan to use your internet, and how many devices you plan to connect.

**What is fiber internet?**

Fiber internet uses cutting edge, 100% fiber technology. Fiber technology uses light waves instead of electrical signals to transmit data, and fiber optic cable can send higher volumes of information over longer distances than copper cable.

**Is fiber internet good for businesses?**

AT&T Business Fiber provides plans that are fast and cost effective for your business. Additionally, plans with AT&T Business Fiber provide symmetrical internet speeds, meaning close to equal upload and download speeds.
