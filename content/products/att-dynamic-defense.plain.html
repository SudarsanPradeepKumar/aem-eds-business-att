Frequently asked questions

What is AT&T Dynamic Defense?

AT&T Dynamic Defense is a network-embedded security service that uses AI and machine learning to detect and block threats in real time, before they reach your firewall.

How does AT&T Dynamic Defense work?

AT&T Dynamic Defense monitors network traffic using advanced threat intelligence and machine learning algorithms. When threats are detected, they are blocked automatically at the network level, preventing them from reaching your business infrastructure.

What's the difference between Standard, Advanced, and Premium plans?

Standard includes basic threat detection and IP blocking. Advanced adds NextGen firewall capabilities including stateful firewall, geo filtering, and web filtering. Premium includes all Advanced features plus advanced threat prevention, anti-virus, and anti-spyware protection.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes, AT&T Dynamic Defense Advanced and Premium are available with two months free for eligible customers with AT&T Dedicated Internet.

Does AT&T Dynamic Defense require additional hardware?

No, AT&T Dynamic Defense is built into the AT&T network, so there's no additional hardware required at your location.
