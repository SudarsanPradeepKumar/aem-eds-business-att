## Frequently asked questions

### Which AT&T business wireless plan is best for my small business?

The best plan depends on your team's needs. Business Unlimited Performance is ideal for heavy data users who need premium features. Business Unlimited Plus offers a great balance of features and value. Business Unlimited Starter is perfect for budget-conscious businesses with basic needs.

### Can I mix and match different wireless plans for my employees?

Yes, you can mix and match AT&T business wireless plans to meet individual employee needs. This flexibility allows you to optimize costs while ensuring each team member has the right level of service.

### What is included in AT&T's 5G business coverage?

AT&T 5G is included with all business unlimited plans at no extra charge. 5G access requires a 5G-capable device and is available in select areas. Coverage continues to expand nationwide.

### Can I bring my current phone number to AT&T Business?

Yes, you can transfer your existing business phone numbers to AT&T. The porting process typically takes 1-2 business days, and we'll help you through every step.

### What business security features are included?

All AT&T business wireless plans include AT&T ActiveArmor mobile security, which provides spam call blocking, fraud call alerts, and other security features to protect your business communications.
