## Frequently asked questions

**Does AT&T have cybersecurity services?**

Yes. Our portfolio of advanced security solutions seamlessly integrates across your existing infrastructure to protect network data—and keeps your network performing at its best.

**How can AT&T Dynamic Defense help protect my network?**

As connectivity continues to evolve, cybersecurity threat detection and mitigation become increasingly complex and costly for businesses. AT&T Dynamic Defense embeds security directly into the network. This empowers our customers with security capabilities to block and tackle threats in real-time before reaching their network. Plus, with its easy-to-use portal, customers can manage security settings, adjust Allow Lists, update IP blocklists, and administer security policies seamlessly.

**What is cybercrime?**

Cybercrime refers to the growth of illegal activities carried out using digital technology, such as fraud, identity theft, and privacy violations. These crimes involve exploiting weaknesses in computer systems and networks to gain unauthorized access and cause harm to people, organizations, and various systems.

**How can we protect ourselves from cybercrime?**

To protect your business from cybercrime:
- Train team members on internet usage best practices to prevent cyberattacks.
- Develop a comprehensive cybersecurity strategy to safeguard against cyberthreats.
- Use automated cybersecurity tools like AT&T Dynamic Defense, which integrates directly into your network.
