Frequently asked questions

What is AT&T 5G for business?

AT&T 5G for business is a collection of services that provides access over the AT&T 5G network including Internet Air for Business, 5G+, On-Premises Cellular Network, Cell Booster Pro, and Private Cellular Network.

What are 5G business internet plans offered by AT&T?

AT&T offers two 5G business internet plans: AT&T Internet Air for Business (fixed wireless internet) and AT&T Wireless Broadband (mobile internet for remote connectivity).

What's the difference between AT&T 5G and AT&T 5G+?

AT&T 5G has wider coverage using low-band spectrum, while AT&T 5G+ provides faster speeds in high-traffic areas using high-band spectrum but with limited availability.

What can 5G do for business?

AT&T 5G delivers high speeds, low latency, and reliable connections that support smooth video calls, quick data transfers, and real-time insights. It also powers IoT and edge computing solutions.
